  • Unified communications and collaboration systems
  • Network security and segmentation design

Equipment Specifications

Structured Cabling Design & Consulting

Our structured cabling consulting services provide comprehensive design, documentation, and project management for enterprise-grade cabling infrastructure. We ensure your physical network foundation supports current and future technology requirements with standards-compliant designs.

Our Approach

  • Copper and fiber optic cabling system design
  • Standards compliance (TIA/EIA, ISO/IEC, BICSI)
  • Cable pathway and space planning
  • Testing, certification, and documentation

Design Documentation

Complete cabling layout drawings, riser diagrams, and floor plans with cable routing specifications.

Bill of Materials

Detailed component specifications including cables, patch panels, faceplates, and containment systems.

Testing & Certification

Comprehensive test results and certification reports for all installed cabling links.

As-Built Documentation

Final documentation package with labeling schemes, port schedules, and maintenance guidelines.
We design to TIA-568, ISO/IEC 11801, and EN 50173 standards, ensuring your infrastructure meets international best practices for performance and reliability.
Yes, we design solutions using Cat6/Cat6A/Cat7 copper cabling and single-mode/multi-mode fiber optics depending on bandwidth requirements and distances.
Absolutely. We conduct thorough site surveys to assess existing pathways and design optimal solutions that work within building constraints.

  • Video conferencing and collaboration platforms
  • Digital signage and IPTV distribution

Room Acoustics

Control Programming

Commissioning & Training

System tuning documentation, user training materials, and maintenance guides.

Data Center Design & Infrastructure Planning

We provide comprehensive data center consulting services from concept design through commissioning. Our expertise covers facility planning, power and cooling design, rack layout optimization, and infrastructure lifecycle management.

Our Expertise

  • Data center facility planning and design
  • Power distribution and UPS system design
  • Precision cooling and airflow management
  • Rack layout and cable management optimization

Concept Design

High-level facility layout, power/cooling architecture, and capacity planning documentation.

Detailed Engineering

Complete MEP drawings, equipment specifications, and infrastructure design packages.

Commissioning Support

Testing protocols, commissioning scripts, and integrated systems testing documentation.

Operations Handbook

Standard operating procedures, maintenance schedules, and escalation matrices.
We design data centers from Tier I through Tier IV based on Uptime Institute standards, tailoring the resilience level to your business requirements and budget.
Yes, we work on greenfield data center projects as well as upgrades and expansions of existing facilities.
Energy efficiency is core to our design philosophy. We target optimal PUE values through hot/cold aisle containment, free cooling, and efficient power distribution.
