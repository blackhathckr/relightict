Join Our Team

At Relight ICT Consulting, we are always looking for talented professionals who share our passion for ICT infrastructure excellence. We offer a dynamic work environment where innovation meets practical problem-solving, and where every team member contributes to shaping India's digital infrastructure landscape.

Why Work With Us

  • Work on prestigious projects across India
  • Collaborative and growth-oriented culture
  • Exposure to cutting-edge ICT technologies
  • Professional development and certifications

Current Openings

Senior ICT Design Engineer

Mumbai / Bangalore | 5+ years experience in structured cabling and network infrastructure design. BICSI RCDD certification preferred.

Data Center Consultant

Mumbai | 3+ years experience in data center design and operations. Knowledge of Uptime Institute standards essential.

Security Systems Designer

Bangalore | 3+ years in electronic security system design. Experience with CCTV, access control, and fire detection systems.

Project Coordinator

Mumbai | 2+ years in ICT project coordination. Strong organizational skills and client communication abilities required.

How to Apply

Send your resume and a cover letter to careers@relightict.in with the position title in the subject line. We review all applications and respond within 5 business days.

Our process includes an initial phone screening, followed by a technical assessment, and a final interview with the leadership team. The entire process typically takes 2-3 weeks.
We offer a hybrid work model. While site visits and client meetings require physical presence, design and documentation work can often be done remotely.
We support and sponsor industry certifications including BICSI RCDD, CDCP, CDCS, and various vendor certifications relevant to your role.
